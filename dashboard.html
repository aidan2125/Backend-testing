Cape Town

Visit these popular destinations in Cape Town:

V & A Waterfront

At the V & A Waterfront in Cape Town, visitors can enjoy:

  • Shopping & Dining: Over 450 shops and 80 restaurants.
  • Two Oceans Aquarium: See marine life like sharks and penguins.
  • Zeitz MOCAA: Contemporary African art museum.
  • Cape Wheel: Panoramic city and mountain views.
  • Robben Island Tours: Historic ferry trip to Mandela’s prison.
  • Harbor Cruises: Scenic boat rides.
  • Battery Park: Outdoor fun with skateboarding and paddling.
  • For more info, click here.

Table Mountain

At Table Mountain, visitors can enjoy:

  • Ride the Cableway: Take the rotating cable car for stunning views on the way up.
  • Hiking: Explore scenic trails like Platteklip Gorge or Skeleton Gorge.
  • Enjoy the Views: Take in panoramic views of Cape Town, the ocean, and surrounding mountains.
  • Photography: Capture breathtaking landscapes and wildlife.
  • Spot Wildlife: Look out for dassies (rock hyrax), birds, and unique plant life.
  • Picnicking: Relax and have a snack at the top with a view.
  • Abseiling: For thrill-seekers, try abseiling off one of the world’s highest commercial descent points.
  • For more info, click here.

Johannesburg

Explore these popular destinations in Johannesburg:

Gold Reef City

At Gold Reef City in Johannesburg, visitors can:

  • Ride thrilling theme park rides like the Anaconda and Tower of Terror.
  • Take an underground mine tour and see gold pouring demos.
  • Play at the trampoline park, laser tag, and arcade.
  • Watch movies in a Victorian-themed cinema.
  • See live shows at the Lyric Theatre.
  • Try their luck at the casino.
  • Dine at various restaurants.
  • Visit the Apartheid Museum nearby.
  • For more info, click here.

Sun City Resort

At Sun City, visitors can:

  • Relax at the Valley of Waves water park.
  • Go on a Big 5 safari in Pilanesberg Game Reserve.
  • Play golf at world-class courses.
  • Explore the Maze of the Lost City.
  • Try adventure activities like zip-lining and quad biking.
  • Enjoy casino games and nightlife.
  • Treat yourself at the spa.
  • Shop and dine at various spots.
  • Have fun with family-friendly attractions.
  • For more info, click here.

Mpumalanga

Explore these popular destinations in Mpumalanga:

Kruger National Park

At Kruger National Park, visitors can:

  • Go on a Big 5 safari (lion, leopard, elephant, rhino, buffalo).
  • Take a self-drive or guided game drive.
  • Join a bush walk for a close-up wildlife experience.
  • Enjoy birdwatching with over 500 bird species.
  • Stay in lodges or campsites surrounded by nature.
  • Visit rest camps with shops, restaurants, and picnic spots.
  • Capture wildlife photography.
  • Join night drives to spot nocturnal animals.
  • For more info, click here.

Mac Mac Falls

At Mac Mac Falls in Mpumalanga, visitors can:

  • View the Twin Waterfalls – a stunning 65-meter-high split waterfall.
  • Swim at Mac Mac Pools – natural rock pools perfect for cooling off.
  • Enjoy a picnic in scenic designated areas.
  • Hike the Secretary Bird Trail – a 3 km trail great for nature and birdwatching.
  • Try canyoning – guided adventures with hiking, swimming, and rock pool jumps.
  • Explore nearby attractions like Berlin Falls, Lisbon Falls, and Pilgrim’s Rest.
  • For more info, click here.

Durban

Explore these popular destinations in Durban:

uShaka Marine World

At uShaka Marine World in Durban, visitors can:

  • Explore Sea World Aquarium – see sharks, dolphins, and penguins.
  • Have fun at Wet ‘n Wild – enjoy water slides and splash zones.
  • Try Ocean Walker – walk underwater with a special helmet.
  • Go shark diving or snorkeling – safely get close to marine life.
  • Test your skills at the Rope Adventure Park.
  • Take a gondola canal ride for a relaxing experience.
  • Shop and eat at Village Walk, styled like a traditional village.
  • Let kids play in Kids World, filled with fun activities.
  • For more info, click here.

Umgeni River Bird Park

At Umgeni River Bird Park in Durban, visitors can:

  • Walk through aviaries to see a wide variety of birds in natural settings.
  • Watch free-flight bird shows held daily at 11 am and 2 pm.
  • Join bird feeding sessions for up-close experiences.
  • Take beautiful photos of exotic birds and waterfalls.
  • Learn from educational exhibits about bird species and conservation.
